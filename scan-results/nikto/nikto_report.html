High Risk Vulnerabilities

Permissive CORS Configuration

HIGH
Business Impact: Allows any website to make cross-origin requests to your application, potentially exposing sensitive data to malicious sites.
Attack Scenario: Malicious websites can perform authenticated requests on behalf of users, leading to data theft or unauthorized actions.
Evidence:
access-control-allow-origin: *
Technical Details: The application sets the Access-Control-Allow-Origin header to wildcard (*), allowing any origin to access the resource.

Missing X-Content-Type-Options Header

HIGH
Business Impact: Browsers may interpret files as different MIME types, potentially leading to XSS attacks through content type confusion.
Attack Scenario: Attackers can upload files that browsers interpret as executable content instead of their intended type.
Recommendation: Add X-Content-Type-Options: nosniff header to prevent MIME type sniffing.
